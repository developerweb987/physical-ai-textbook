# Introduction to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the comprehensive textbook on Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics. This module provides an overview of the field and sets the foundation for the rest of the course.

## What You'll Learn

This textbook covers the essential concepts, theories, and practical implementations needed to understand and develop physical AI systems and humanoid robots. We'll explore everything from fundamental robotics principles to advanced control strategies.

## Course Structure

The textbook is organized into progressive modules:

- **Module 1**: Introduction to Physical AI concepts
- **Module 2**: Theoretical foundations
- **Module 3**: Locomotion and manipulation
- **Module 4**: Perception and control systems
- **Module 5**: Advanced topics in multi-robot systems and ethics
- **Module 6**: Applications and implementation strategies

## Getting Started

Begin with the fundamentals and progress through each module sequentially for the best learning experience.
