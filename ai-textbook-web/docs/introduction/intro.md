# Introduction to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the **Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics** textbook. This comprehensive resource covers the essential concepts, theories, and practical implementations needed to understand and develop physical AI systems and humanoid robots.

## About This Textbook

This textbook is organized into progressive modules, each building upon the previous to provide a comprehensive understanding of physical AI and humanoid robotics:

- **Module 1**: Introduction to Physical AI concepts
- **Module 2**: Theoretical foundations in robotics and AI
- **Module 3**: Locomotion and manipulation systems
- **Module 4**: Perception and control systems
- **Module 5**: Advanced topics including multi-robot systems and ethics
- **Module 6**: Applications and implementation strategies

## Getting Started

Begin with the fundamentals and progress through each module sequentially for the best learning experience. Each module contains detailed explanations, practical examples, and exercises to reinforce your understanding.
